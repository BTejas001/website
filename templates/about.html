Grand Finale - Smart India Hackathon 2023

As the team leader, I successfully guided our team to the Grand Finale of the prestigious Smart India Hackathon 2023. Our innovative project, MediTrain, was recognized for its potential to revolutionize medical training for trainee doctors. Here are the details of the project:

  • MediTrain (Software for Trainee Doctors): A virtual platform designed to help medical trainees enhance their skills by working with simulated patient cases.
  • Features of MediTrain include:
    • Comprehensive virtual test cases for various diseases, complete with symptoms and causes.
    • A self-learning tool to help users identify diseases and practice treatments virtually.
    • An engaging and interactive interface to simulate real-world medical challenges.
  • My contributions included:
    • Conceptualizing the project idea and dividing tasks among team members.
    • Developing the user interface and backend logic to manage virtual patient simulations.
    • Ensuring the seamless integration of all components and presenting the project effectively during the event.
